Consent Management

Consent is the permission given by a user to share or use their personal or sensitive data for a specific purpose, time period, and with defined conditions.

What is Consent?

Consent ensures that data is accessed only after the data owner clearly agrees to how their data will be collected, shared, and used.

Why Consent is Important

  • Protects user privacy
  • Builds trust between data providers and consumers
  • Ensures legal and policy compliance
  • Prevents unauthorized data usage

Key Elements of Consent

  • Purpose – why the data is needed
  • Duration – how long the consent is valid
  • Data Scope – what data is shared
  • Access Control – who can access the data
